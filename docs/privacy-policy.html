DocFlow – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026  |  Effective immediately

Introduction

DocFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a PDF utility application developed by UK Solutions. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using DocFlow you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

DocFlow is a locally-processed app. All PDF and image operations happen entirely on your device. We do not upload your documents to any server.

  • Files you select – PDFs and images are read temporarily to perform the requested operation (merge, split, compress, convert, protect, unlock) and are never transmitted off your device.
  • Device permissions – We request storage/media access solely to let you pick files and save results. Camera access is used only for scanning documents when you choose that option.
  • Usage & analytics data – We use Google Firebase Analytics (anonymised, aggregated) to understand which features are used so we can improve the app.
  • Advertising identifiers – We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (GAID) and related signals in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

How We Use Information

  • To provide PDF processing features entirely on-device.
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To monitor app stability and crashes via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns.

Third-Party Services

DocFlow uses the following third-party SDKs, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – advertising
  • Google Firebase – analytics & crash reporting

Data Retention & Security

We do not store your documents. Temporary copies created during processing are deleted immediately after the operation completes. Advertising identifiers are managed by Google and subject to their data-retention policies. We apply industry-standard practices to protect any data processed within the app.

Children's Privacy

DocFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke file or camera permissions at any time in Android Settings → Apps → DocFlow → Permissions.
  • You can opt out of personalised ads via Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
  • You may contact us to request information about any data we hold related to your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
